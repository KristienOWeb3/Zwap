Deposit. Buy. Withdraw.

Whether you're converting crypto to naira or buying tokens with a wallet address — ZWAP makes both feel as simple as a bank transfer.

Deposit & Auto-Convert

Send any supported crypto to your ZWAP address. The moment it lands, ZWAP converts it to naira automatically — no button, no waiting. Under 60 seconds.

Buy Crypto by Wallet Address

Enter any destination wallet address, pick the network, pay from your naira balance. Crypto lands in that wallet. No seed phrases. No connect-wallet. Just like a bank transfer.

Withdraw or Spend

Withdraw naira to any bank account in seconds, or spend globally with your ZWAP Visa card in 200+ countries. Zero manual conversion at checkout.

FAQ

Common questions

ZWAP is a crypto & fiat payments platform that automatically converts incoming cryptocurrency to local naira the moment it arrives. You can also buy crypto by wallet address, save in stablecoins, and withdraw to any bank account.
Send any supported crypto to your ZWAP deposit address. We detect it on-chain, convert to naira at the live rate, deduct 0.85%, and credit your balance — within 30–90 seconds. No action from you.
Navigate to Buy Crypto. Select a token, enter the amount, type in any destination wallet address, choose the network, and confirm. ZWAP deducts from your naira balance and sends crypto directly to that wallet. No wallet creation inside ZWAP. No seed phrases needed.
18+ tokens including USDT, USDC, BTC, ETH, BNB, SOL, MATIC, ARB, TRX, XRP, DOGE, TON, AVAX, BUSD, DAI, OP, LTC and more — across TRC-20, ERC-20, BEP-20, Base, Solana, Polygon and more networks.
Crypto deposits: 0.85% off-ramp fee. Buying crypto: 0.50% platform fee + exact network fee at cost. Naira withdrawals: ₦10 (up to ₦200K) · ₦50 (₦200K–₦1M) · ₦100 (above ₦1M). Naira deposits always free. Minimum withdrawal: ₦100.
ZID is your ZWAP Identification Tag — an alias replacing your legal name on transaction records. Counterparties see "ZID-XXXXXX" not your name. All users are fully KYC-verified.
Yes. Full KYC (BVN, NIN, government ID). Funds held with licensed banking partners. Real-time fraud monitoring. ZWAP never stores your private keys — your external wallet is always yours.
